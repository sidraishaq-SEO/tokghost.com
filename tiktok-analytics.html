FAQ

TikTok analytics questions

Can I see TikTok analytics for accounts that are not mine?
Yes, for public accounts. TokGhost reads publicly visible engagement data such as likes, views and follower counts, and calculates averages and engagement rate from it.
What is a good engagement rate on TikTok?
It varies by size and niche, but engagement rate is a far better signal than follower count. A smaller account with high engagement often has more real influence than a large, passive one.
Will the account owner know I checked?
No. Viewing public stats through TokGhost leaves no trace.
Do I need to log in?
No. Enter a username and view. No account, no sign-up.
Is it free?
Yes. The analytics viewer is free with no limits.
It says the account was not found. What now?
Check the spelling of the username, and make sure the account is public. If it still fails, refresh the page, clear your browser cache, or try a different browser.
The tool is not loading. How do I fix it?
Refresh the page first. If that does not help, clear your cache and cookies, try another browser, and check your internet connection. TikTok also changes often, so a feature can break briefly while we update it.
