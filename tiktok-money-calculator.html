FAQ

TikTok money calculator questions

How much does TikTok pay per 1 million views?
Through the Creator Rewards Program, reported payouts vary widely, often from around twenty dollars to several hundred per million views, depending on audience location, watch time and engagement. Most creators earn far more from sponsorships than from platform payouts.
How accurate are these estimates?
They are directional, not exact. The calculator reads public engagement data and applies typical rates. Real earnings depend on sponsorships, niche, and deals we cannot see.
How is profile value estimated?
It combines follower count, average engagement and typical rates for the niche. Treat it as a ballpark figure for negotiation, not a valuation you would take to a bank.
Why do finance and tech accounts earn more?
Advertisers pay more to reach those audiences, so the same view count is worth more in a high-value niche than in a general entertainment one.
Is it free?
Yes. The money calculator is free with no limits.
It says the account was not found. What now?
Check the spelling of the username, and make sure the account is public. If it still fails, refresh the page, clear your browser cache, or try a different browser.
The tool is not loading. How do I fix it?
Refresh the page first. If that does not help, clear your cache and cookies, try another browser, and check your internet connection. TikTok also changes often, so a feature can break briefly while we update it.
