Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

This privacy policy explains how TokGhost handles information when you use our tools. In short: we collect as little as possible, and we do not require you to log in or share personal details.

  • We do not keep a record of the usernames, profiles or videos you look up.

We may also display advertising, and advertising partners such as Google AdSense may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings, and you can learn how Google uses data at its own policy pages.

Third-party links

Our site may link to third-party pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites, so please review their policies separately.

Children

TokGhost is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top reflects the latest version. For any privacy question, reach us via the contact page.
