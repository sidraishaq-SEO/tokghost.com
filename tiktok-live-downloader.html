FAQ

TikTok live downloader questions

Can I download a TikTok live stream?
Yes. Enter the username of someone streaming now, set a recording length, and TokGhost captures the live as it airs, then hands you an HD file.
How long can I record?
Up to five minutes per recording. You set the exact length before you start.
Does the recording have a watermark?
No. The saved file is clean HD with no logo across it.
What happens to my file afterwards?
It is automatically deleted from our servers ten minutes after it is created. Download it before then.
Can I record a live that already ended?
No. A live can only be recorded while it is actually streaming. Once it ends, it cannot be captured.
Is it free?
Yes. Recording lives is completely free with no limits.
It says the account was not found. What now?
Check the spelling of the username, and make sure the account is public. If it still fails, refresh the page, clear your browser cache, or try a different browser.
The tool is not loading. How do I fix it?
Refresh the page first. If that does not help, clear your cache and cookies, try another browser, and check your internet connection. TikTok also changes often, so a feature can break briefly while we update it.
