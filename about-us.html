About TokGhost

Last updated: 11 July 2026

TokGhost is a privacy-first toolkit for viewing and saving public TikTok content without an account. We built it for a simple reason: people should be able to look at public profiles, stories and videos without their name being logged, and without signing into anything.

What we do

TokGhost lets you view TikTok profiles, watch stories and lives, read comments, browse followers and reposts, and download videos, photos and audio, all anonymously. Everything runs in your browser. There is nothing to install and no account to create.

How we handle privacy

We only access publicly available TikTok data. We do not ask for your TikTok login, we do not store personal information, and we do not keep a record of what you view or download. Your activity stays yours.

Our approach

Many tools in this space oversell what they can do or bury you in ads. We take a different line: a clean, fast interface, honest descriptions of what each tool can and cannot do, and no clutter between you and the content you came for.

Independent from TikTok

TokGhost is an independent service. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to TikTok or ByteDance in any way. The name TikTok is used only to describe the content our tools work with. If you have questions, reach us through our contact page.
