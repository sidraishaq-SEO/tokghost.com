FAQ

TikTok story viewer questions

Will the account owner know I viewed their story?
No. TikTok only logs story views made through its own app while you are signed in. TokGhost loads stories through its own servers, so your name never appears on the owner's viewer list.
Can I download TikTok stories?
Yes. You can save any story you view as an HD file, in original quality with no watermark, before it expires after 24 hours.
Do I need to log in?
No. Enter a username or profile link and start watching. You never sign in or share any personal details.
Can I view stories from private accounts?
No. Only public accounts are accessible. If an account is set to private, its stories cannot be viewed through any tool.
How long are stories available?
TikTok stories stay live for 24 hours after posting. View or download them within that window, since they are gone once it closes.
Is it free?
Yes. The story viewer is completely free with no limits on how many accounts you can check.
