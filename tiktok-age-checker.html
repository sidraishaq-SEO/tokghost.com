FAQ

TikTok age checker questions

How can I tell how old a TikTok account is?
Enter the username and TokGhost estimates the creation date from public profile signals, then shows how long the account has been active.
Why does account age matter?
A very new account with a huge following is a common sign of bought followers. Age helps you judge whether growth is real.
Is the date exact?
It is an estimate. TikTok does not publish exact creation dates, so the tool infers it from public signals.
Do I need an account?
No. Enter a username and check. No login required.
Is it free?
Yes. The age checker is free with no limits.
It says the account was not found. What now?
Check the spelling of the username, and make sure the account is public. If it still fails, refresh the page, clear your browser cache, or try a different browser.
The tool is not loading. How do I fix it?
Refresh the page first. If that does not help, clear your cache and cookies, try another browser, and check your internet connection. TikTok also changes often, so a feature can break briefly while we update it.
